Nomenclature used by LipidLynxX

The Nomenclature used by LipidLynxX is based on the widely used "Shorthand notation for lipid structures" published by Gerhard Liebisch, et al. (2013).

Additional features for modified lipids including ordered controlled vocabularies and lipid levels matrix are introduced by LipidLynxX to perform cross level matching.

Lipid Level Matrix

Lipid levels defined by three sub levels allow cross level matching of lipids including residues levels, modification levels, and double bond levels .
